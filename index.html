The New Farm Woodland Forum

For all people

The Farm Woodland Forum is the place for those interested in agroforestry, whether you're a farmer, researcher, student, or just curious.

The root of information

The Farm Woodland Forum is now a singular location for all your agroforestry information for the UK. This includes news, webinars, farm walks, relevent articles and much more.

Pinpoint agroforestry near you

Seeing is believing, so find out where the nearest agroforestry farm is to you and get in contact or look out for open days and farm walks.

Sharing is caring

Find links to other organisations relevent to agroforestry around the UK, Ireland and Europe. Also, explore the database of student and farmer agroforestry projects
